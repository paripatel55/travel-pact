Design Thinking Prototype

Trip Alignment Pact

A lightweight prototype to test whether making expectations explicit before booking reduces trust and planning friction in student travel groups.

1) Create Trip Room

Start with basic context so your preferences are grounded in one trip scenario.

2) Personal Alignment Questions

Rate each item from 1 to 5. This prototype focuses on three key variables: pace, budget flexibility, and social style.

3) Pick Teammates to Compare

Choose 1-2 sample traveler profiles to simulate group dynamics for your test.

4) Results: Alignment + Trip Pact
