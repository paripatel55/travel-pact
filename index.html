BondVoyage reveals how you tend to travel when a trip gets squeezed by money, timing, coordination, or group tension before you lock in plans.

You get a composite profile, a destination-aware way to compare with other travelers, and a shared alignment pact. Happy, safer trips start with clarity.

1) Create Trip Room

Start with basic context so your preferences are grounded in one trip scenario.

2) Behavioral Scenarios

When money, timing, or group pressure kicks in, people behave differently. You will get 10 randomized scenarios—pick what you would most likely do. There are no right answers.

3) Personal Alignment Questions

Required: Rate each item from 1 to 5. We focus on three key variables: pace, budget flexibility, and social style.

Preference Details (Optional but Recommended)

Optional input, max 3 items each. Add comma-separated items. Examples are included to help you start quickly.

4) Discover Travelers & Compare

Select 1–2 people to compare with your profile. Cards are sorted by fit with your trip and scores. You can also add someone manually below.

Add Teammate Manually

Use this if someone is not in the list.
